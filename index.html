About Us

The Minnesota Vikings are a professional American football team based in Minneapolis, Minnesota.
They are a member of the NFC (National Football Conference) North division. Since 1961, the Vikings
have won 4 conference championships and 20 divisional championships. Although they have not
won a Super Bowl, the Vikings continue to strive towards that as the ultimate acheivement in the NFL.
The Vikings have an all-time overall record of 524-451-11, which is the best winning percentage of teams
who have not won a Super Bowl. The Vikings are owned by American Billionaire Zygmunt Wilf and have
been since 2005. Kevin O'Connell is our new head coach who will be starting his Vikings career in the 2022 season.

Notable Vikings

Adrian Peterson

Adrian Peterson was a running back for the Vikings from 2007 to 2016.
Peterson is considered to be one of the greatest running backs in football history.

  • 2012 NFL MVP
  • Named one of the 50 Greatest Vikings
  • 4x first team All-Pro
  • 2012 NFL Offensive Player of the Year
  • 2007 NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year
  • 3x NFL rushing yards leader
  • 2x NFL rushing touchdowns leader

Fran Tarkenton

Fran Tarkenton was a quarterback for the Vikings from 1961 to 1966, and again from 1972 to 1978.

  • 1975 NFL MVP
  • 1x first team All-Pro
  • Named one of the 50 Greatest Vikings
  • 1978 NFL passing yards leader
  • 1975 NFL passing touchdowns leader
  • 1975 NFL Offensive Player of the Year
  • Inducted into the Football Hall of Fame in 1986
